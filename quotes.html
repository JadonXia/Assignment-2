A list of quotations from Tim Berners-Lee

Created by Jadon Xia, 3/19/21



Quotes

Interview for Internet World, 1994:

Anyone who has lost track of time when using a computer knows the propensity to dream, the urge to make dreams come true and the tendency to miss lunch.

Web Accessibility Initiative, 1997:

The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect.

Answers for Young People, post-1999

I just had to take the hypertext idea and connect it to the TCP and DNS ideas and -- ta-da! -- the World Wide Web.

Berners-Lee, T. Net Neutrality: This is Serious, 2006:

When I invented the web, I didn't have to ask anyone's permission. Now, hundreds of millions of people are using it freely. I am worried that that is going end in the USA.

developerWorks Interviews: Tim Berners-Lee

I think in general it's clear that most bad things come from misunderstanding, and communication is generally the way to resolve misunderstandings, and the Web's a form of communications, so it generally should be good.

Speech from Knight Foundation, 2008:

The Web does not just connect machines, it connects people.

Seminar on the Web's future, pre-2009:

The Web as I envisaged it, we have not seen it yet. The future is still so much bigger than the past.
